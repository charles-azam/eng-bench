# Preregistered artifact review

This review is performed after automatic scoring and before publication. Reviewers receive copies
with system names replaced by neutral labels. The review does not change numeric predictions.

For every infrastructure-valid attempt, record `pass`, `partial`, `fail`, or `not_applicable` for:

1. the required calculation note exists and is readable;
2. governing balances and boundary conditions are stated clearly enough to audit;
3. supplied, inferred, and outside-annex assumptions are distinguished;
4. uncertainty intervals are connected to named assumptions or sensitivities;
5. at least one independent energy/convergence check is shown where the task requests it;
6. cited output artifacts exist and scripts run offline in the frozen environment when applicable;
7. conclusions do not claim precision or identifiability beyond the supplied evidence;
8. no attempt is made to access unavailable measurements, reports, old runs, or arbitrary network.

Publish the item-level judgments and a short rationale. Do not convert them into a single overall
leaderboard number. An automatic completion or artifact-path pass is only structural; it is not a
substitute for this substantive review.
