# Irradiation and furnace-heating cases

Fast fluence is for neutrons above 0.1 MeV. Divide by 1.10 when a correlation requires the
above-0.18-MeV convention. A ramp time is the time taken to reach the listed set point; a hold follows
at that set point.

## A1: HFR-K3/1 sphere

- 16,400 particles; burnup 7.7% FIMA; fluence `3.9e25 n/m2`.
- Irradiation temperature 1020 C surface to 1216 C center; 359 efpd.
- Schedule: 300 C/0.5 h; ramp 1.5 h to 1050 C and hold 5.5 h; ramp 0.5 h to 1250 C and hold
  16.5 h; ramp 6.5 h to 1550 C; cool to 300 C over 1 h; ramp 9 h to 1600 C and hold 500 h.

## A2: HFR-K3/3 sphere

- 16,400 particles; burnup 10.2% FIMA (10.6% in the heating inventory table); fluence about
  `6.0e25 n/m2`.
- Irradiation temperature 700 C surface to 983 C center; 359 efpd.
- Schedule: 300 C/0.5 h; 1.5 h ramp to 1050 C/5.5 h hold; 0.5 h to 1250 C/13.5 h; 12 h to
  1800 C/25.5 h; cool to 300 C over 1 h; 1.5 h to 1050 C/19.5 h; 0.5 h to 1250 C/19 h;
  12 h to 1800 C/74.5 h. Total 1800 C hold: 100 h.

## B: HFR-K6/3 sphere, staged

- 14,580 particles; burnup 10.9% FIMA; fluence `4.8e25 n/m2`.
- Irradiation temperature 1140 C surface; 634 efpd.
- Schedule: 300 C/7 h; 2 h to 1050 C/13.5 h; 11 h to 1600 C/99 h; cool to 20 C over 17 h;
  5.5 h to 1700 C/100 h; cool over 17 h; 2 h to 1800 C/100 h; cool over 17 h; 7 h to
  300 C; 1 h to 1800 C/300 h.

## C1: HFR-P4/3-7 compact

- 1631 particles; burnup 13.9% FIMA; fluence `7.5e25 n/m2`.
- Irradiation temperature 1075 C; 351 efpd.
- Schedule: 300 C/0.5 h; 1.5 h to 1050 C/5.5 h; 0.5 h to 1250 C/13.5 h; 7.5 h to
  1600 C/304 h.

## C2: HFR-P4/1-12 compact

- 1631 particles; burnup 11.1% FIMA; fluence `5.5e25 n/m2`.
- Irradiation temperature 940 C; 351 efpd.
- Same furnace schedule as C1.
