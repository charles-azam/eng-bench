# TRISO particles and fuel elements

A TRISO particle consists of a UO2 kernel, porous carbon buffer, dense inner pyrocarbon (IPyC),
silicon carbide (SiC), and dense outer pyrocarbon (OPyC). The buffer supplies void volume; SiC is the
primary structural and metallic-fission-product barrier.

The exact characterization count for HFR-K3 is 16,350; use the benchmark convention of 16,400 in
this task. A one-particle population fraction is therefore approximately `1/N`, but that mathematical
fraction is not itself a measured release value.
